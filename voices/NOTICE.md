# Preset voice attribution

The preset reference clips in this folder are **real human recordings in the
public domain**, released under **CC0 1.0** (no rights reserved). They come from
[LibriVox](https://librivox.org/) public-domain audiobook readings, curated,
noise-reduced, and trimmed for zero-shot text-to-speech use by the
[Voice-Zero](https://github.com/OwenTyme/voice-zero) project (CC0).

CC0 requires no attribution, but we keep the trail anyway — credit to the readers:
